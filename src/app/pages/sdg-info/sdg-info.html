Key Targets

What SDG 11 Aims to Achieve

11.1

Ensure access to safe, adequate, and affordable housing and basic services for all by 2030.

11.2

Provide access to safe, affordable, and sustainable transport for all — especially vulnerable groups.

Relevant to this app
11.3

Enhance inclusive and sustainable urbanization through participatory city planning and management.

11.b

Increase the number of cities adopting disaster risk reduction strategies aligned with the Sendai Framework.

Why It Matters Here

How This App Supports SDG 11

Baguio City's jeepney system is the backbone of daily commuting for thousands of residents and students. Yet navigating its terminals has always relied on word-of-mouth knowledge — leaving newcomers, tourists, and vulnerable commuters behind.

By digitizing terminal locations and routes, this guide directly advances Target 11.2: making public transport accessible to all, regardless of familiarity with the city.
